Images play a vital role in visualizing complex ideas, illustrating examples, and enhancing understanding. They can include diagrams, charts, infographics, and illustrations that supplement the text and provide visual context.

To simply insert an image into an HTML component, you can click the image button on the editor toolbar of a text component and this will open up a dialogue box allowing you to simply drag your image in to upload it or to search for and select one of your previously uploaded images.
